## 📚 Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

-   **Plain English:** AI systems that can independently take actions and make decisions to achieve a goal, rather than just responding to direct commands.
-   **Why it matters:** This represents a shift from reactive AI assistants to proactive AI "workers" that can automate entire workflows, not just individual tasks, offering significant efficiency gains for businesses.
-   **Business example:** An agentic AI might autonomously manage a client's social media presence, from generating posts to scheduling and responding to comments, or navigate e-commerce sites to find and purchase products.
-   **Sound fluent:** "We're seeing a big push towards **agentic AI**; it's about enabling systems to proactively handle tasks rather than just waiting for prompts."

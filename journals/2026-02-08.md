## üìö Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

-   **Plain English:** An AI system that can autonomously reason, plan, and execute multi-step tasks to achieve a goal, often interacting with other tools or systems, rather than just responding to a single prompt.
-   **Why it matters:** Agentic AI moves beyond simple chatbots to proactive "AI coworkers" that can manage complex projects, making AI a more integrated and powerful part of business operations. This signifies a shift from AI as a tool to AI as a partner.
-   **Business example:** An agentic AI could independently research market trends, draft a business report, create accompanying presentation slides, and then schedule a meeting to present the findings, all without constant human intervention.
-   **Sound fluent:** "The industry is rapidly moving towards **agentic AI**, where systems can autonomously complete complex workflows, not just one-off tasks."

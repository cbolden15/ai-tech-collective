## 📚 Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

- **Plain English:** AI systems that can independently plan, execute, and often self-correct multi-step tasks to achieve a goal, acting with a degree of autonomy.
- **Why it matters:** It shifts AI from being a tool that responds to prompts to a proactive entity that can manage entire workflows, dramatically increasing automation potential for businesses.
- **Business example:** An AI agent autonomously handling customer support from inquiry to resolution, including accessing databases, processing returns, and sending follow-up emails, without human intervention until a complex issue arises.
- **Sound fluent:** "The real shift in AI this year is towards agentic systems; it's no longer just about generating content, but about AI taking action to complete entire business processes."

## 📚 Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

- **Plain English:** AI systems that can understand a goal, break it down into steps, and then autonomously execute those steps across different tools and environments to achieve the goal, often learning and adapting along the way.
- **Why it matters:** This represents a significant leap from reactive chatbots to proactive, task-oriented AI, allowing for much greater automation of complex workflows and decision-making.
- **Business example:** An agentic AI could autonomously manage a small business's entire marketing campaign: researching trends, drafting ad copy, generating visuals, scheduling posts, and optimizing ad spend across platforms, all with minimal human oversight.
- **Sound fluent:** "We're moving beyond simple prompts; the real game-changer is **agentic AI**, where systems can actually *do* tasks for you, not just answer questions."

## üìö Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

- **Plain English:** AI that can perform multiple steps autonomously to achieve a goal, rather than just responding to a single prompt. It can plan, execute, and monitor its own actions, often interacting with other tools and systems.
- **Why it matters:** This shift moves AI from a reactive tool to a proactive assistant, capable of handling complex workflows and significantly increasing automation in business operations.
- **Business example:** An agentic AI could autonomously manage a client onboarding process: sending welcome emails, setting up CRM entries, scheduling initial calls, and creating project folders, all without human intervention after the initial trigger.
- **Sound fluent:** "The latest agentic AI models are transforming how small businesses handle their operations, moving beyond simple chatbots to truly autonomous workflow execution."

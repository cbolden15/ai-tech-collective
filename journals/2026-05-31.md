## 📚 Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

-   **Plain English:** AI systems that can understand a high-level goal, break it down into smaller steps, execute those steps using various tools (like browsing the web or interacting with other software), and learn from the outcomes to achieve the objective, often with minimal human intervention.
-   **Why it matters:** It moves AI beyond just answering questions or generating content to actively performing tasks and automating complex, multi-application workflows, fundamentally changing how businesses operate.
-   **Business example:** A real estate agent's **agentic AI** assistant (like Gemini Spark) autonomously researches new property listings based on client criteria, drafts personalized email alerts, schedules virtual tours, and updates the CRM—all from a single instruction.
-   **Sound fluent:** "The shift to **agentic AI** means we can automate entire workflows, not just individual tasks, truly empowering small business owners."

## 📚 Term of the Day: Industry Fluency

**Term:** `Agentic AI`

-   **Plain English:** AI systems that can plan and execute complex, multi-step tasks autonomously, often interacting with other software and tools, without constant human supervision.
-   **Why it matters:** This represents a significant evolution beyond simple AI assistants, enabling automation of entire workflows and decision-making processes, shifting the focus from individual AI tools to interconnected AI systems.
-   **Business example:** An agentic AI system could not only draft a marketing email but also identify target audience segments, schedule the email campaign, track performance, and report results, all without explicit human instruction for each step.
-   **Sound fluent:** "We need to move beyond simple prompts and start thinking about **agentic AI** to truly automate our lead generation process."
